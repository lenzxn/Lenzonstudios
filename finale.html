WHO ARE WE?

Xplore Our Sound
Step into Lenzon Studios and immerse yourself in a world where sound, vision, and creativity comes alive.
Our team is ready to bring your ideas to life.

Founded in 2015 by music producer Ethan Wells and creative director Maya Hartman, our company—EchoPoint Studios—was born in the heart of Los Angeles, CA. What began as a small recording studio has grown into a full-service creative hub for artists. Our mission is simple: to empower creators with cutting-edge recording facilities, expert management, and bold visual direction. With a team of top-tier engineers, legal advisors, and artistic visionaries, we offer a seamless experience that nurtures creativity from the studio to the stage, helping artists bring their vision to life.

At Creative Harmony Productions, we provide tailored management solutions designed to support artists and creative talents. Our dedicated team offers strategic guidance and marketing support to help you reach your full potential. With customized business plans, we navigate the complexities of the music and entertainment industry together, allowing you to focus on what you love most: creating.

Studio Recording
In our state-of-the-art recording studio, equipped with the latest technology, we provide an inspiring and creative environment for musicians and producers. Our experienced sound engineers are passionate about sound quality and work collaboratively with you to capture your music in the best possible way. Whether you're a solo artist or a band, we ensure that your sound is perfected, making your project a true masterpiece.

Visual Creativity
Our visual creativity department serves as a platform for design, animation, and film production. We specialize in creating unique and memorable visual experiences that effectively capture and communicate your message. From graphic design and brand identity to film and animation, we work with you to bring your vision to life and create a strong visual presence that resonates with your audience.
